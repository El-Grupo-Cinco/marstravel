About us

Our company

Mars Travel was established in 2025 by four tech-enthusiasts with a passion for space and astronomy. As a new commercial space race emerged, co-founder Domingos Mojica secured funds from various investors to finance our project. Meanwhile in 2026 Ninis Blomerus started developping the spaceships technology from her garage while Zever Jemil designed the Mars Travel city. As our inhouse technology advanced, Franklin Eck was working on the space suits and race rover safety implementation.

In the summer of 2027, we started to send material to Mars to start building the resort. The construction was achieved by our fleet of heavy-lifter drones, working day and night to finish the construction in less than six months.

April 7, 2028 marked an historical event, both for mankind and for Mars Travel, as the first interplanetary tourist spaceship took off from SpaceX Starbase in Boca Chica, Texas.

The rest is history:

  • 2029: first Mars Grand-Prix
  • 2029: first martian rave
  • 2029: famous chef and former rap artist Eminem opens our Michelin rated restaurant
  • 2030: first opera on Mars it was Carmen by the clone of Celine Dion
  • 2030: we hosted the peace summit that ended all wars on Earth (Eminem's famous martian recipes helped convince the French delegations)
  • 2031: the four original founders of Mars Travel test the Olympus Mons climb after finishing the installation of the path to the top. this made them the first space mountaineers ever!

Our values

At Mars Travel we stand for peace on Earth and love for all. The founders various origins, reaching from Portugal to Sweden to New Caledonia, build the founding principles for tolerance and hope for the future. We work continously to improve the future on Mars but also on Earth. All our technology is open source and has help prevent the devastating tsunami on the Nigerian coast in 2029.

Mars Travel has a strict safety culture. Since we started in 2025 we have had no accident. Or quality system are considered among the best in the world.

When booking a Mars Travel trip, you contribute to the improvment of mankind.
