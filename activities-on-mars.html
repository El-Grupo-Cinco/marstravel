The jumping track

JUMP AROUND!

At one fourth of Earth’s gravity, you are going to go far! Jump four to six meters in the air or throw a 20 kg lead ball 20m away, test your new super powers!
Next to the city, train on our track field with our experienced coaches. Why not finishing the day in town at our sport club’s sauna.

We warmly recommend pole vaulting since you could easily beat the Earth record.

Come back to Earth a gold medalist!

Rover racing

3, 2, 1 RACE!

Are you feeling the need... the need for speed?
Come try your skills at the race rover track: an 10 km off-road path next to town.
Our electrical rovers can manage speeds in the 100KPH (62MPH) in a straight line.
The track offer jumps and sharp turns.
With Olympus Mons in the background, the scenery is fantastic!
So buckle up and race through the red dust in our state-of-the-art rover. Safety is guaranteed thanks to all-embracing airbag, the same technology used on the old probes landing on Mars in the 2000s.
Sunday is Grand Prix day, during which you can compete and win a free dinner at one of our luxurious restaurant. Alternatively, you can watch the race back in the city.
